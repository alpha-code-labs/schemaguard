# v1.5 — Deferred from v1

The following capabilities were evaluated during v1 planning and
explicitly deferred. Each is tracked either in `docs/things_to_fix.md`
(implementation notes) or in `docs/tasks.md` (Milestone 9 and v1.5
items). Nothing here was forgotten — every item was considered and
scoped out of v1 with a specific rationale.

## dbt downstream impact detection (Milestone 9)

The secondary dbt capability — parsing a `manifest.json` to detect
downstream model breakage — was the Milestone 9 deliverable. Per
`docs/DECISIONS.md` the packaging decision (task 9.0) and the
ship-or-defer decision (task 9.5) are resolved as: **deferred to
v1.5.** Milestones 1–8 shipped without dbt code, dependencies, or
analyzer hooks. The v1 JSON schema (`schemaVersion: "1"`) does not
include a `downstream_impact` group. When dbt ships in v1.5, the
schema version will bump and the group will be added.
